What's in the bottle?

Bottle No. 001 - 012:

  • Started: 10/1/17 Mixed: 11/26/17 Bottled: 12/17/17
  • 20 lemons were used. Sourced from Surendra and Jeannie's house.
  • 1.75 L of Everclear alcohol (75.5% Alc/vol - 151 proof)
  • Syrup composed of 6 cups of distilled water and 4.5 cups of pure cane sugar.

About Me

While visiting Capri, Italy, in September, 2017, Angela and I took a cooking class at Ristorante Michel'angelo, learning how to make ravioli, lemon torte, and limoncello. Upon our return to the US, I started making limoncello to reminisce about that wonderful afternoon and share a bit of the sweet warmth of Capri with family and friends.

Thank you for taking the time to visit the website and I hope you enjoy your bottle of limoncello!
